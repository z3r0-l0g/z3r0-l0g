### 🌑 Who We Are
Zero Log is the premier hub for **Cybersecurity** and **Technical Excellence** at our campus. We are a collective of developers, hackers, and builders dedicated to mastering the digital landscape. From B.Tech CSE students to tech enthusiasts, we operate where the code meets the wire.

### 🛠️ What We Do
* **Vulnerability Research:** Hunting for exploits and understanding system weaknesses.
* **CTF Training:** Preparing our squad for global Capture The Flag competitions.
* **Infrastructure:** Building and maintaining tools that power our campus tech ecosystem.
* **Workshops:** Hands-on sessions on everything from NetSec to AppSec.
